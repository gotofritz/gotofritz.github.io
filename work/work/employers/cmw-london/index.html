CMW London

I joined in September 2007, to help establish what was then CMW Interactive, provide technical expertise and development know-how.

CMW London is an integrated agency, part of the Media Square group. Their main digital clients were Cadbury and Transport for London.

As a technical director my duties included

  • Technical architecture for both in-house and outsourced projects

  • Drafting technical documents

  • Liaising with clients during pitching, scoping, and deployment

  • Brain storming and pitch preparation

  • Hands-on DHTML, Actionscript, PHP, .NET, SQL development

  • Data extraction and tracking, liaising with data analysts

After a year the company was restructured, CMW Interactive was integrated with the rest of the agency, and I moved on.

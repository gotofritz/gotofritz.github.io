Image / Video Gallery

A few of the projects I worked on had a gallery element to them.

For E.ON Talking Energy we used a 3D Flash video component to display YouTube videos.

The E.ON YouTube channel uses a Papervision based video carousel to display videos from the YouTube API.

The Moto Z8 project was a full-on YouTube video gallery clone built in Flash.

Both the LCR and London Shard sites have a simple image gallery within the news section, content managed by the client.

Hair Histories was basically a gallery of images uploaded by users - limited to one image per user, and with a competition element.

The Foyer website had an user generated gallery section.

• Anxious? Upset? Lonely? Bored? The screen has what you need
  • Paw at the screen like it's a slot machine. Trigger those hits of dopamine
  • Your digital devices are tools to be used purposefully, not slot machines to be slapped over and over for your amusement.
  • Cut back on your aimless browsing. Way back.
  • Don't treat digital like a cheap way to fill dead air with mindless clicking. This is like eating out of boredom.
  • Reintroduce friction. Denature the "whip-out-the-phone” impulse.
  • When you aren't using your phone, put it away—in a bag or on a stand on a table across the room
  • When you aren't using your laptop, close it and put it in a drawer. Create separation.
  • SLOW DOWN. Try thoughtfully stopping… opening your bag… removing your phone… mindfully using it… putting it back.

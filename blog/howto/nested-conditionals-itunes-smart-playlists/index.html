Nested Conditionals in iTunes Smart Playlists Have Gone?

2012-07-22, Sun
HowTo
Comments

In the spirit of “fixing things that aren’t broken”, Apple have now removed nested conditional rules for iTunes smart playlists.

Or rather, made them less obvious - they are still there, you need to click the alt key to turn the + buttons next to a rule into an …, which allows you to create a rules subgroup. Once created it can then be dragged around.
